Usability Study

Method

My portfolio website was studied. The target audience is teenagers. The subjects found information on the index page, went through the photo album on the Graphics page, and clicked a button on the Javascript page.

Results

All subjects were able to complete each task. However, they all struggled with the first question due to the wording.

Discussion

There were a few critical comments. One subject thought that the margins should be slightly larger. However, it was generally well-received; two subjects liked the color scheme, and they all agreed that the website was "generally good" for the most part.
