Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: Ling's Cars

Purpose of graphics on this site

The graphics on the home page of Ling's Cars are flashy and bright, which serve to grab the visitor's attention. At first glance, the website seems very busy and cluttered - there are many seemingly random and pointless images spread throughout that have nothing to do with selling cars. However, if anything, this makes the website more intriguing and entertaining to navigate. Each image serves to provide a fun car buying experience.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics fit the purpose, organization, and style of the page
  2. No - Large grpahics have not been avoided
  3. No - No central graphic
  4. No - Overly bright and flashy images are repeatedly used
  5. No - Graphics with textual content information is included
  6. No - Textual alternatives are not used
  7. Yes - Sufficient contrast between text and background color
